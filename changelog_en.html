v1.0.0

May 2026 · Initial release

Added

  • Cross-platform LAN transfer across iOS, macOS, Android, Windows, and Linux
  • Live Photo cross-platform transfer with motion preserved
  • Motion Photo support for Huawei, Xiaomi, OPPO, and vivo formats
  • Resume transfers automatically after network drops or device sleep
  • Open ZIP / RAR archives directly inside the app
  • Send the same batch to multiple devices in parallel
  • Accounts: sign in with Apple, Google, or email verification code
  • Cross-network relay: securely transfer between devices on different networks
  • AirTok Plus subscription and one-time topup packs (iOS only)
