Changelog

What's new, improved, and fixed in each release

v1.0.0

May 2026 · Initial release

Added

  • AirTok Plus monthly subscription: 50 GB of cross-network relay traffic per month, billed every 30 days from your subscription anchor day
  • One-time topup packs: +10 GB / +50 GB, valid 30 days, stacks on top of your existing quota
  • Accounts: Sign in with Apple ID, Google, or email verification code; the same email across providers automatically merges into one account
  • Account deletion: In-app account removal on iOS / Android (App Store and Play Store compliance)
  • Cross-platform LAN transfer: iOS, macOS, Android, Windows, Linux all interoperable
  • Live Photo cross-platform transfer: Motion track preserved end-to-end, native restoration on the receiver
  • Motion Photo support: Lossless interop with Huawei, Xiaomi, OPPO, vivo motion photo formats
  • Resume transfers: Auto-resumes after network drops or device sleep, no restarting from scratch
  • In-app archive extraction: Open ZIP / RAR archives directly inside the app
  • Multi-device parallel send: Push the same batch to multiple devices at once
  • TLS 1.3 encryption: Encrypted in transit + certificate fingerprint pinning (TOFU) against MITM

Design

  • Apple-minimal paywall: five core benefits + subscription/topup dual track
  • Account page subscription card: remaining quota, renewal date, topup entry at a glance
  • Email verification: 30-second resend countdown persists across screens to prevent duplicate requests
  • Quota display uses decimal GB (consistent with Apple, Google, and ISP conventions)

Stability

  • iOS background → foreground daemon restart storm fully addressed (two-layer defense)
  • Receiver progress bar no longer starts from 0 (resume baseline pre-counted)
  • Device card progress binding hardened against empty IP edge cases
  • Extensive ICE negotiation tuning and log noise reduction
