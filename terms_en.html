2. Service Description

AirTok is a local network file transfer tool that provides the following core features:

  • Transferring files, photos, videos, and text between devices on the same local network
  • Cross-platform Live Photo / Motion Photo transfer and restoration

  • TLS encrypted transfers and device trust management

The App does not require an internet connection (except for version update checks). All transfers are completed within the local network.

Most of the App is free forever — including LAN transfers, cross-platform delivery, and Live Photo handling. On iOS, AirTok Plus is offered as a paid subscription primarily to raise your cross-network relay (TURN) quota and unlock related convenience features.

AirTok Plus Monthly Subscription

  • Product: AirTok Plus monthly subscription (com.airtok.send.plus.monthly)
  • Price: as displayed in-app (Apple shows the final locale-adjusted price including applicable tax at checkout)
  • Benefits: 50 GB of cross-network relay traffic per month, unlimited Live Photo saves with motion preserved, no per-file size limit
  • Billing cycle: billed every 30 days from your subscription anchor day, NOT aligned to calendar month. For example, subscribing on May 15 means renewal on June 15, with quota resetting on the same day.

One-Time Topup Packs

  • Products: +10 GB pack (com.airtok.send.topup.10gb), +50 GB pack (com.airtok.send.topup.50gb)
  • Type: Consumable IAP, one-time purchase
  • Validity: 30 days from purchase (independent of subscription state — non-subscribers may also buy)
  • Consumption: Stacks on top of your current monthly quota. The system burns subscription quota first; once that is exhausted, topup balances are consumed in soonest-to-expire order (FIFO).
